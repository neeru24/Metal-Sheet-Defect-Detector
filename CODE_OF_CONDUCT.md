# 🛡️ Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct — Metal-Sheet-Defect-Detector

---

## 🌟 Our Pledge

We, as members, contributors, and maintainers of the **Metal-Sheet-Defect-Detector** project, pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are committed to fostering an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy environment for learning and collaboration.

---

## 🚦 Our Standards

### Positive behaviors we encourage:
- 💬 Showing empathy and kindness to others
- 🤝 Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- 📝 Providing constructive feedback
- 🌱 Owning up to mistakes and learning from them
- 🎯 Keeping discussions focused on the project’s goals

### Unacceptable behaviors include:
- 🚫 Sexualized language or imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention
- 🛑 Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, or personal attacks
- ⚠️ Harassment (public or private)
- 🔒 Publishing private information without permission
- ❌ Any behavior that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## 👨‍⚖️ Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards and ensuring compliance with this Code of Conduct. They may take any necessary action, including editing or removing contributions that violate these rules, and issuing warnings or bans when appropriate.

---

## 🌐 Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — including GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any related online or offline interactions where someone is representing the project.

---

## 🛡️ Reporting


All reports will be handled promptly, fairly, and with respect for the privacy of the reporter. Please contact me through the issue section of the repository

---

## 📖 Enforcement Guidelines

1. **✏️ Correction**
   - *Impact*: Use of inappropriate language or behavior deemed unprofessional.
   - *Consequence*: Private warning with clarification; public apology may be requested.

2. **⚠️ Warning**
   - *Impact*: Single incident or repeated unacceptable behavior.
   - *Consequence*: Official warning and restricted interactions for a period of time.

3. **⏳ Temporary Ban**
   - *Impact*: Serious or ongoing violation.
   - *Consequence*: Temporary exclusion from project participation.

4. **🚫 Permanent Ban**
   - *Impact*: Repeated or extreme violations (e.g., harassment or hate speech).
   - *Consequence*: Permanent removal from all project spaces.
